Privacy Policy  ·  Last updated: April 24, 2026

KoreanTalk ("we", "our", "us") is developed by Shoktree. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and how we use it when you use the KoreanTalk mobile application ("App").

By using the App you agree to this policy. If you do not agree, please uninstall the App.

1. Information We Collect

Minimal by design

We collect only the minimum data needed to operate the App.

a) Device Identifier
A random identifier is generated locally on your device when you first open the App. It is never linked to your name, email, or any other personal detail. It is used only to enforce daily usage limits and prevent API abuse.

b) Conversation Content
Text messages you send during practice sessions are transmitted to our API server (hosted on Vercel) and forwarded to Anthropic's Claude AI to generate responses. We do not store conversation content on our servers after the response is returned. Anthropic may retain data per their own Privacy Policy.

c) Usage Data (stored locally on device)
Session count, minutes practiced, and ads watched are stored only on your device using AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly share it.

d) Purchase Information
In-app purchases and subscription status are handled by RevenueCat. We receive anonymous entitlement data (e.g. "user has Pro"). Payment details are never shared with us. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

e) Advertising Data
We use Google AdMob to serve ads to free-tier users. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data for ad personalisation, subject to your consent (see Section 4). See Google's Privacy Policy.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To deliver AI-powered conversation practice responses
  • To enforce daily free-usage limits (rate limiting)
  • To process and verify in-app purchases
  • To display relevant advertisements to free-tier users
  • To improve App performance and fix bugs (via aggregated, anonymous crash reports)

We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for marketing purposes.

3. Data Sharing

We share data only with the following third-party service providers, solely to operate the App:

  • Anthropic — AI response generation (Privacy Policy)
  • Vercel — API hosting (Privacy Policy)
  • RevenueCat — Subscription management (Privacy Policy)
  • Google AdMob — Advertising (free tier only) (Privacy Policy)

We do not share data with any other third parties.

4. Advertising & Consent (GDPR / ATT)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or Switzerland, we will ask for your consent before serving personalised ads, in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and Google's EU User Consent Policy.

On iOS, the App requests permission to track you across apps and websites using Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework. You may deny this permission; the App will continue to function, but ads may be less relevant.

You can withdraw consent at any time in your device Settings.

5. Data Retention

  • Conversation messages — Not retained on our servers after response delivery.
  • Device identifier & rate-limit counters — Retained for up to 90 days, then automatically deleted.
  • Local app data — Retained on your device until you uninstall the App or clear app data.

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the EEA or UK, you have the right to:

  • Access — Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Deletion — Request erasure of your data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Portability — Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Objection — Object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Withdraw consent — Withdraw any consent you have given at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at kyw1984@gmail.com.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 (or under 16 in the EEA). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Security

All data transmitted between the App and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS. We follow industry-standard security practices to protect your information. No method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.
